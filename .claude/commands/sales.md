## Pricing Principles for Indie Tools

1. **Simple tiers** - Max 3 pricing options
2. **Generous free tier** - Let users fall in love
3. **Clear upgrade trigger** - Obvious when you need Pro
4. **Annual discount** - 2 months free typical
5. **No feature confusion** - Easy to understand
