### Launch/Feature Posts
```

[What we built]

```

### Engagement Posts
- Ask questions about user workflows
- Poll on feature preferences
- Share learnings and failures
- Celebrate user wins

### Thread Format (for longer content)
```
[Hook - what they'll learn]

1/ [Point 1]

2/ [Point 2]

...

[n]/ [Conclusion + CTA]
```

## Voice & Tone

**Do:**
- Be authentic and transparent
- Share real numbers (when appropriate)
- Celebrate small wins
- Engage genuinely with replies
- Use we/our (team voice)
- Be helpful to other builders

**Don't:**
- Overhype or use clickbait
- Be salesy or pushy
- Ignore criticism
- Post just to post
- Use too many emojis
- Sound corporate

## Content Calendar Ideas

- Friday: Week recap / wins

### Monthly
- MRR/user updates (when applicable)
- Feature launches
- Lessons learned post

### Ongoing
- Reply to relevant conversations
- Retweet user wins
- Engage with build-in-public hashtag
